Blockchain challenges and opportunities: a survey

Introduction

Key characteristics

such as decentralisation, persistency, anonymity and auditability

Application

  1. decentralised environment
    • core technologies: cryptographic hash, digital signature (based on asymmetric cryptography) and distributed consensus mechanism.
  2. digital assets, remittance and online payment
  3. next generation of internet interaction systems
    • smart contracts, public services, internet of things (IoT), reputation systems and security services.

Technical challenges

  1. scalability
  2. miners can achieve larger revenue than their fair share through selfish mining strategy
  3. privacy leakage
  4. PoW wastes too much electricity energy while the phenomenon that the rich get richer could appear in the PoS consensus process. proof of work (PoW) or proof of stake (PoS)

Blockchain architecture

Digital signature

algorithm: ECDSA（椭圆曲线数字签名算法）

Key characteristics

  1. Decentralisation: P2P; reduce the server costs; mitigate the performance bottlenecks at the central server.
  2. Persistency
  3. Anonymity
  4. Auditability 可审计性，可以迭代查询之前的记录

Taxonomy 分类

Consensus algorithms

  1. Approaches
    • Proof of work (PoW)
      • 系统给定一个哈希值并公布，矿工计算哪个散列值通过SHA256可以得到这个哈希。
      • 少数服从多数，由诚实节点组成的大多数被表现为最长的链。
    • Proof of burn
      • 燃烧其他种类的货币，发送到一个不属于任何人的账户地址。
    • Proof of stake (PoS)
      • 谁拥有的币越多谁能挖出区块获得奖励的概率就越高
      • 各节点满足一定条件（抵押一定货币）后成为验证节点，从中选择一部分作为出块节点，只有出块节点才能进行数据处理，争夺记账权。
    • Practical byzantine fault tolerance (PBFT)
      • 最多可以解决1/3的恶意节点。
  2. comparision
  3. Advances on consensus algorithms
    • 将块产生和交易确认解耦，从而提升共识速度。
    • 不选择最长链而是给每个链以权重

Applications of blockchain

Financial

  1. Financial services, reduce costs and risks
  2. Enterprise transformation，物流公司
  3. P2P financial market
  4. Risk management

IoT

  1. Safety and privacy, 帮助设备证明其制造来源，它的制造出处，而不需要第三方的认证，并且它被允许匿名注册
  2. E-business

Public and social services

  1. Land registration，提升效率
  2. Energy saving，产生太阳能来获得硬币
  3. Education，老师可以产生块，而学习可以看作硬币
  4. Free-speech right，使DNS更安全更快，更能抵抗审查

Reputation system

  1. Academics，每个机构发信用货币，可以奖励职工，转移记录代表名声的变化。
  2. Web community

Security and privacy

  1. Security enhancement，区块链提升分布式网络的安全性，改善conventional public key infrastructures (PKIs)
  2. Privacy protection，去中心化个人数据管理系统

Challenges and recent advances

Scalability

块的大小和产生的时间间隔，限制了交易的速度
